Frequently Asked Questions

What is Agro-Alert?
Agro-Alert is a system designed to monitor environmental conditions relevant to agriculture, such as temperature, humidity, light intensity, and soil moisture. It provides real-time data and alerts to help optimize crop growth.
How does Agro-Alert work?
Agro-Alert uses sensors to collect data from the environment. This data is then transmitted to a central system, where it is processed and displayed on a user-friendly dashboard. Users can access this dashboard through a web browser or mobile app.
What sensors are used?
The system uses a combination of temperature and humidity sensors, light-dependent resistors (LDRs) for light intensity, and soil moisture sensors.
Is there a mobile app available?
Yes, there is a mobile app available for both Android and iOS devices. You can download it from the Download App page.
How can I download the data?
You can download the sensor data in CSV format from the dashboard page. There is a "Download CSV" button available.
How often is the data updated?
The data is updated regularly, ensuring you have access to near real-time information about your environment. The exact update frequency depends on the configuration of the sensor system.
Who do I contact for support?
For support or any questions, please contact us at support@agroalert.com
